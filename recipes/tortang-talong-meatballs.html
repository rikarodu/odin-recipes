Tortang Talong Meatballs

Description

Tortang Talong Meatballs are awesome. You can enjoy eating it by itself, or you can make certain dishes using it. Tortang Talong Meatballs is a type of meatball composed of eggplant and ground pork. This makes a perfect appetizer. It is crisp on the outside and it tastes yummy.

Ingredients

  • 4 pcs Eggplant
  • 0.25 kg Ground Pork
  • 2 tbsp Onion
  • 3 cloves Toasted Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Scallion
  • 1 sachet Maggi Magic Sarap 8g
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Pierce eggplant with a fork and roast over open flame until charred. Set aside in a bowl and cover. Once cooled, peel off charred skin and coarsely chop. 15mins
  2. Combine eggplant, ground pork, onion, garlic and spring onion. Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Mix well and form into meatballs. 15 mins
  3. Pan fry meatballs in oil until golden brown. Serve immediately. 20 mins
