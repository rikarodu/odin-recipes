Hotdog Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 1 pc Carrot
  • 2 pcs Hotdog
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 2 pcs Egg
  • 3 cups Cooked Rice
  • 1 sachet Maggi Magic Sarap 8g
  • 0.5 cup Cabbage

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, carrot and hotdog in oil. 10mins
  2. Push on the side and pour beaten eggs. Stir and cook until barely set. 5 mins
  3. Add rice and season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Pour 2 tbsp of water on the side and gently fluff until steaming hot. Stir in cabbage. Transfer on a serving plate and serve. 5 mins
